Reviewer checklist · pre-publish

  1. Story 1 · Hero — countdown end-time matches launch time exactly; @sgcoalition mention resolves.
  2. Story 1 · Hero — "GREY WAVE" / "Coalition 'Grey Wave' Wallet 1/2" / "$35" all fit inside top safe zone.
  3. Story 2 · Detail — back.jpeg has #000 black overlay at bottom 35% before any copy is placed.
  4. Story 2 · Detail — poll options ≤ 24 chars, mutually exclusive, on-brand.
  5. Story 3 · Scarcity — countdown end-time in IG-recognized timezone; poll "Should we run 2/2? / Yes / Wait" pulling demand signal.
  6. Story 3 · Scarcity — "1 OF 2" reads BEFORE "$35" in the visual order.
  7. Story 4 · Manifesto — wallet texture at 10% opacity never competes with "TRUST YOURSELF." copy.
  8. Story 5 · CTA — link sticker URL == PDP URL (no trailing slash, no redirect typo).
  9. Story 5 · CTA — "SHOP NOW →" + Link sticker + Mention sticker do not overlap.
  10. All slides — copy stack has safe-zone clearance: nothing crosses into the top 250 px or bottom 320 px.
  11. All slides — live preview on actual Android/iOS IG account before publishing (treat Canva preview as soft only).
  12. All slides — thread publishes in this exact order: Hero → Detail → Scarcity → Manifesto → CTA.
